Fun Facts About Animals

Interesting facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry
  2. Camel can stay without water for many days
  3. Camel is called Ship of Desert

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleon can change colour
  2. Chameleons are reptiles
  3. Most Chameleons have a prehensive tail that use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeon can be of differnt colours
  2. Pigeon can be trained
  3. Pigeons like to stay near human beings

Interesting facts about teddy

  1. The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall!
  2. The world's largest stitched teddy bear is proudly American!
  3. The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.

Interesting facts about panda

  1. They have great camouflage for their environment.
  2. Bamboo is critical to their diet
  3. They spend a lot of their day eating.

Interesting facts about flying-cicada

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  2. The body of a cicada is similar to that of a violin or a guitar
  3. They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil
